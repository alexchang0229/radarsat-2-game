RADARSAT-2: The Game

About

A game inspired by RADARSAT-2.

Use your mouse to aim the radar beam and hold spacebar to collect acquisitions.

Press A, S, D to change beam width.

An acquisition less than 50% collected results in a dataloss, 3 datalosses is game over.

This game is a fun personal project and not associated with MDA Space or the Canadian Space Agency
